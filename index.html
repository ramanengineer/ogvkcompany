About ogvk.org

ogvk.org is a public Twitch organization open to everyone! Launched as a mega community free for all, anyone can join through our simple selection process with no strings attached. Based in New Zealand (NZST/NZDT), we offer 24/7 live streaming with talents like xwlasxd and xvbrznem, fostering a vibrant, inclusive environment for streamers and viewers alike.

Our mission is to create a welcoming space for all Twitch enthusiasts. Whether you're a streamer, viewer, or supporter, ogvk.org is your home. Join our community on Discord and explore the opportunities below!
